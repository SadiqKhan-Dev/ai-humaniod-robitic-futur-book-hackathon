# Chapter 5: Future Possibilities

Over the next decades, humanoids may become common in various fields.

### 5.1 Space Exploration

Humanoids can work in dangerous environments on:

- Mars
- The Moon
- Deep-space missions

### 5.2 Rescue and Disaster Response

They can survive fires, floods, radiation, and collapse zones better than humans.

### 5.3 Smart Cities

Traffic, energy use, security, and public services may integrate humanoid agents.

### 5.4 Household Companions

Humanoids could eventually:

- Cook meals
- Drive vehicles
- Provide emotional companionship
- Support mental health
