# Chapter 2: Understanding Humanoid AI

Humanoid AI refers to robots that replicate human characteristics.

### 2.1 What Makes a Humanoid?

A humanoid typically includes:

- Human-shaped body
- Movable joints resembling muscles
- Sensors for vision, sound, and touch
- AI systems for reasoning and decision-making
- Emotional or expressive capabilities

### 2.2 Current Humanoid Robots

Several advanced humanoids exist today:

- **Ameca** — best facial expressions and conversations
- **Atlas (Boston Dynamics)** — exceptional mobility and balance
- **Optimus (Tesla)** — designed for factory work
- **Sophia** — the first robot with official citizenship

### 2.3 Why Build Humanoids?

Humans designed environments for humans.
Humanoid robots can work in:

- Homes
- Offices
- Factories
- Hospitals
- Dangerous zones

No redesign of buildings or tools is needed.
