# Chapter 3: Impact on Society

Humanoid AI will dramatically influence our daily lives.

### 3.1 Future of Work

Humanoids will assist in:

- Manufacturing
- Construction
- Logistics
- Household chores

Human jobs will shift from manual labor to supervision, creativity, and management.

### 3.2 Education

Students will learn from AI tutors that adapt to their pace and style.

### 3.3 Healthcare

Humanoid caregivers can support elderly people, deliver medicine, monitor health, and assist in emergencies.

### 3.4 Everyday Life

Humanoid assistants may help with cooking, cleaning, babysitting, and personal tasks.
