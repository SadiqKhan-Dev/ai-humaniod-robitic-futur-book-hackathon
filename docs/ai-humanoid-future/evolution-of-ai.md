# Chapter 1: Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

### 1.1 Rule-Based Systems

### 1.2 Machine Learning

Machines learned patterns from data instead of following fixed rules.

### 1.3 Deep Learning

Neural networks enabled computers to analyze images, sound, and complex information with human-like accuracy.
