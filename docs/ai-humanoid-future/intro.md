# Introduction to the AI-Humanoid Future

Welcome to the future. This textbook explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics, and the future they will shape together.

In this textbook, we will cover:

*   The history and evolution of AI and robotics.
*   The current state of the art in humanoid robotics.
*   The ethical considerations of creating and living with AI humanoids.
*   The future of work, society, and humanity in the age of AI humanoids.

Let's begin.
